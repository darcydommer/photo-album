Camera Icon for Favicon

This is a simple camera icon SVG that can be used as a favicon for the Photo Album website.

To use as favicon:

  1. Take a screenshot of this SVG
  2. Crop it to just the camera icon
  3. Resize to 32x32 or 16x16 pixels
  4. Save as favicon.ico or favicon.png
  5. Place in the root directory of your website

Alternatively, you can use an online favicon generator by uploading the screenshot of this SVG.
